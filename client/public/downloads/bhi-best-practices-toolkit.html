Description: Team-based approach with care coordinators and psychiatric consultants

  • Behavioral Health Care Manager: Direct patient care and monitoring

Patient Screening and Assessment Tools

üìã Primary Screening Instruments

Depression Screening

  • PHQ-9: 9-item depression severity measure (administered quarterly)
  • PHQ-2: 2-item brief screener for annual wellness visits
  • GAD-7: Generalized anxiety disorder assessment

Substance Use Screening

  • AUDIT-C: 3-item alcohol screening tool
  • DAST-10: Drug abuse screening test
  • SBIRT: Screening, brief intervention, and referral protocol

Cognitive Assessment

  • MoCA: Montreal Cognitive Assessment for dementia screening
  • Mini-Cog: 3-minute cognitive screening test
  • Clock Drawing Test: Simple executive function assessment

üß† Depression Management Protocol

Initial Assessment (Week 1-2)

  1. Complete PHQ-9 and risk assessment
  2. Review medication history and medical conditions
  3. Assess psychosocial stressors and support systems
  4. Develop collaborative treatment plan

Treatment Options

Severity Level PHQ-9 Score Recommended Treatment
Mild 5-9 Watchful waiting, self-management support
Moderate 10-14 Antidepressant medication + behavioral activation
Severe 15-19 Combination therapy + frequent monitoring
Very Severe 20-27 Immediate specialist referral + safety planning

Follow-Up Schedule

  • Week 2-4: Initial response assessment
  • Week 6-8: Treatment adjustment if needed
  • Week 12: Response evaluation and plan modification
  • Monthly thereafter: Maintenance monitoring
