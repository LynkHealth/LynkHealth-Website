Documentation Requirements

üìã CCM Documentation Standards

Required Elements for Every Claim

  • Patient Eligibility: Two or more chronic conditions documentation
  • Comprehensive Care Plan: Must be shared with patient/caregiver
  • 24/7 Access: Documentation of continuous care availability
  • Care Coordination: Record of communication with other providers
  • Time Tracking: Exact start/stop times for all activities

Care Plan Components

  1. Problem List: All chronic conditions with ICD-10 codes
  2. Expected Outcomes: Specific, measurable treatment goals
  3. Treatment Options: All interventions considered
  4. Care Instructions: Patient self-management guidance
  5. Medication Management: Complete drug therapy review
  6. Community Resources: Available support services

üì± RPM Documentation Standards

Device Requirements

  • FDA Approval: Medical device must have 510(k) clearance
  • Data Collection: Minimum 16 days of readings per month
  • Data Transmission: Automatic transmission to provider
  • Clinical Review: Provider interpretation of all data
